About

Hello. I am Sven, a coder, philosopher and visual artist with an interest in all things beautiful and/or witty. My interests are cinema, theatre, philosophy of science, and left-wing politics.

The purpose of this website is to showcase some of my work online. Depending on which side of me interests you most, I suggest you explore either the code, art or academia section. If you are enjoying this experience, then I can recommend visiting other places where I have made my mark. You can find me on Facebook, Github, Researchgate, Imdb, Google Scholar, YouTube, and also in real life - occasionally.

Sven Delarivière is a doctor in philosophy, and part of the Centre for Logic and Philosophy of Science (CLPS) of the Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB), Belgium.

Interests: collective epistemology, philosophy of mind, feminist epistemology, cognitive science, free will
